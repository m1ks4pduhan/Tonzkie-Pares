Food Information


Beef Pares

We take pride in serving a true Filipino favorite—tender beef, slow-cooked to perfection in a rich, sweet, and savory soy-based sauce. Paired with fragrant garlic rice and a comforting bowl of hot broth, our Beef Pares isn’t just food; it’s a classic that brings warmth and satisfaction with every bite. Carefully balanced with just the right blend of flavors, it’s the perfect meal for any time of the day.

Beef Mami

A steaming bowl of rich, slow-simmered broth, tender beef slices, and perfectly cooked noodles—it’s the perfect balance of hearty and comforting. Finished with fresh green onions and crispy garlic, every spoonful is packed with warmth and flavor.

Fried Rice

Golden, flavorful, and perfectly cooked, our fried rice pairs well with any dish, adding an extra layer of deliciousness to every meal.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Crunchy and packed with seasoned meat filling, our Lumpiang Shanghai is the perfect balance of crispy, juicy, and satisfying.

Toge

A simple yet flavorful dish of stir-fried mung bean sprouts, carrots, and tofu, cooked with a savory blend of seasonings.
